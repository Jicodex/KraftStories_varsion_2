Beginner-Friendly Experience

What this class is

This is a live, guided Turkish mosaic lamp workshop led by Nur, a Turkish designer and educator joining you from Hawaii.Over approximately 2 hours, participants will:

  • Learn the basics of Turkish mosaic lamps and their connection to Islamic art, sacred geometry, and light.

  • Follow a clear step-by-step process to design and build a mosaic lamp using hand-cut glass tiles and beads.

  • Experience a calm, meditative creative flow.

The class is designed to be relaxing, non-intimidating, and beginner-friendly, even for people who say they’re “not artistic.”

Hands-On Creation

What participants will create

By the end of the session, each participant will:

  • Create their own Turkish-style mosaic lamp using a glass globe, colorful tiles, and glass beads.

  • Choose from guided patterns or improvise their own designs.

  • Set a personal intention—like flexibility, acceptance, peace, or growth—and infuse that intention into their lamp as they work.

Participants leave with a functional lamp (nightlight-level brightness) they can assemble, display, and continue to work on after the class
